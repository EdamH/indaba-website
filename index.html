IndabaX Tunisia 2023

Join us for 2 days packed with panels, workshops, hackathons, talks and more in the 4th edition of Deep Learning IndabaX Tunisia! If you’re an AI enthusiast, you cannot miss this event!

May 13th and 14th, 2023

Sup’com, Technopole El Ghazela

About IndabaX 2023 Deep Learning IndabaX Tunisia 2023 is an opportunity for beginners, experts and researchers to communicate, share knowledge and benefit from each other’s experience. The two-day congress will include Keynotes, Hands on workshops and a 20-hour hackathon. For this edition, IEEE Supcom Student Branch and Instadeep have come together to host an event that meets your expectations and offers you a variety of opportunities to enlarge your network and deepen your knowledge.

Hackaton Information

Learn more about the hackaton

In partnership with ZINDI

Zindi is the largest community of african data scientists. It is the first data science competition platform in Africa. Zindi hosts an entire data science ecosystem of scientists, engineers, academics, companies, NGOs, governments and institutions focused on solving Africa’s most pressing problems.

20-hour hackaton for you to test your abilities

The 20-hour hackathon that will be hosted on the Zindi platform and revolves around biotechnologies is an exciting opportunity for data scientists and engineers to come together and tackle real-world problems related to the intersection of biology and technology. Participants in the hackathon will have a limited amount of time to analyze datasets related to biotechnologies
